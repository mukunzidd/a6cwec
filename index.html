    Urban planning and building

    A6CWEC undertakes architectural, structural design and supervision of works.
    Supervision and management of construction projects, detailed drawings for execution, design of structural elements, Elaborate preparation of bills of quantities, site surveying, building permits application and follow up, elaboration of tender documents, projects financial evaluation, Environmental impact assessment and environmental management plan.

    A6CWEC takes on the supervision of road construction projects, traffic volume determination and forecast, Traffic survey, road safety audit, geometric road design. Structural and pavement design for both urban and rural feeder roads.
